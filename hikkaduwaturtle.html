What We Do

Our sole goal at the Hikkaduwa sea turtle hatchery and rescue center is to save the highly endangered turtle species. Started by a group of volunteers, we have thrived towards our goal of becoming a well-known non-profit organization that has succeeded in saving turtles, though our work is not yet complete given that turtle lives are still in danger of extinction.
